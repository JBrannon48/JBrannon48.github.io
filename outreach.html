Outreach

Why we do outreach

Outreach is a core part of who we are as a team. It gives us the chance to inspire younger students, make STEM more accessible, and connect with our community. By sharing what we’ve learned, we grow as mentors and leaders. Outreach helps us live the values of FIRST—gracious professionalism, teamwork, and making a difference beyond the robot.
